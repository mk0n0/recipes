もち | Mochi

Description

もち /mo-CHI/: A Japanese rice cake made up mochigome, a short-grain glutinous rice that gives it an elestic, chewy texture. Mochi tastes like rice on its own but is often paired with a sweet filling such as red bean paste.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen thoughtfully prepared mochi

Preparation Steps

  1. Buy mochi from your favourite konbini.
  2. Try not to eat them all at once.
  3. Enjoy!
