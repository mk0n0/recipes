すし | Sushi

Description

すし /soo͞′shē/: A Japanese dish made of small portions of sticky white rice falvoured with vinegar and usually wrapped in seaweed or topped with vegetables or raw fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocado rolls
  • 2 toro nigiri
  • 2 tamago nigiri
  • 2 ikura nigiri

Preparation Steps

  1. Buy the above rolls and nigiri at your favourite sushi restaurant.
  2. Try not to overstuff yourself.
  3. Enjoy!
