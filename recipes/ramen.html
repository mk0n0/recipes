ラメン | Ramen

Description

ラメン /rä′mən/: A Japanese dish comprised of broth and noodles, often garnished with fresh vegetables, a softboiled egg and bits of meat or plant-based protein.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups reduced sodium vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup miso paste
  • 1 5-ounce package of fresh ramen noodles
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 2-3 handfuls of various vegetables for topping
  • 1 soft-boiled egg

Preparation Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, add oil and heat until fragrant.
  2. Add miso paste and stir to incorporate.
  3. Add the broth and bring to a slow simmer. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  4. Bring another bot of unsalted water to boil over high heat. Cook ramen according to package directions.
  5. Strain noodles and add to a bowl. Ladle the broth over the cooked noodles.
  6. Cut soft-boiled eggs in half and add to bowl.
  7. Garnish with vegetables and scallions.
  8. Enjoy!
