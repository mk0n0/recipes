ラメン | Ramen

Description

ラメン | ra-men: A Japanese dish comprised of broth and noodles, often garnished with fresh vegetables, a softboiled egg and bits of meat or plant-based protein.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups reduced sodium vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup miso paste
  • 1 5-ounce package of fresh ramen noodles
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 2-3 handfuls of various vegetables for topping
  • 1 soft-boiled egg
